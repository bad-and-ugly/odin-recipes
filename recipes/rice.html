Plain white rice

Just your good old regular rice to complement your beans. This recipe will serve four people but you can keep the proportion to make more or less of it.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 bayleaf (optional)
  • 2 cups of water (more or less)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash the rice. This will get rid of excess starch.
  2. Chop the garlic
  3. Add the oil to a medium pan on low heat. Add the garlic and the bayleaf and braise them briefly.
  4. Add the rice to the pan and braise it a little - just mix it well, you want the garlic and oil to get to the rice.
  5. Add enough water to cover the rice.
  6. Add a three-fingered pinch of salt.
  7. Cover the pan with the lid, but only halfway. You don't want too much steam to gather in the pan, nor for the water to dry too quickly.
  8. Cook until the water dries. Careful not to burn it!
