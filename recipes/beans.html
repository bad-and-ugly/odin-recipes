Beans

Cooked beans with tomato and green bell pepper

This is Mexican-style beans. They'll cook with a tomato, an onion and a bell pepper - plus optional bacon!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of beans (red, brown or black)
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 large green bell pepper (or red, or jalape√±o)
  • 1 tablespoon of oil (any kind will do)
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 three-fingered pinch of salt
  • 6 cups of water

Steps

  1. Soak the beans in water for a few hours, or just pressure cook them. They must be cooked in 3 times as much water (volume-wise)
  2. Chop the onion and the garlic. Add the chopped onion to a pan and braise it in the oil for a little. Then, add the garlic and bay leaf keep braising.
  3. Chop the tomatoes and the pepper coarsely, and add it to the pan. Stir until soft.
  4. Add the cooked beans to the tomato and seasonings mixture, and add salt. Stir it, let it heat and mix.
  5. Serve it hot
